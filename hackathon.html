UW Lean Hackathon 2026

A two-day hackathon bringing together students, faculty, and industry partners to work on challenge problems showcasing the power of the Lean Proof Assistant. Each team will include at least one Lean-proficient researcher and one domain expert.

RSVP here

Goals

  • Tackle ambitious cross-disciplinary problems
  • Introduce Lean to a wider audience
  • Formalize mathematical statements and their proofs in Lean with the goal of contributing to Mathlib
  • Build infrastructure and tools to assist formalization
  • Foster collaboration across PL, verification, LLMs, and mathematics
  • Build and deepen industry partnerships
